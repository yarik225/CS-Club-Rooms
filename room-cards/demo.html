Advanced Web Development
Advanced Web Development club is where you develop advanced webs. It requires an understanding of JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. It is recommended that students have at least 6-7 completed web development projects before joining.

Simple Web Development
Simple Web Development club is where you develop simple webs. It does not require any experience. It is recommended that students have about 0.6-0.7 completed web development projects before joining.
